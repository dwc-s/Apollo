Learning mode (root only). Capture a known-good archer to author the reference. This view reports the raw measured angles for each checkpoint so you can transcribe tuned targets into form_checkpoints.py. Nothing is scored or saved here.
{% endif %}

Apollo extracts your skeleton from the footage in your browser and grades your full-draw position against good-form checkpoints for your bowstyle. The video never leaves your device and is never saved — only the measured angles are. For the best read, set the camera behind the shooting line, level with the archer, with your whole body in frame.

Form checkpoints are based on USA Archery’s BEST method (Biomechanically Efficient Shooting Technique).
