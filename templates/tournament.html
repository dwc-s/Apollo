Tournament rounds

Tournament mode runs a structured round with a fixed target face, end size, and arrow count. Apollo locks the target and end size to the round's specification and tallies your score using the official ring radii. Scoring rules follow the published World Archery / NFAA / USA Archery conventions — see documentation/tournament/ for the full reference and known limitations.
