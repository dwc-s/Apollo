Apollo

Apollo is an archery application for logging the data of shooting sessions and analyzing performance.

Sessions & shot logging
  • Tap or click a target image to record each shot's exact location
  • Your arrow's true diameter is always shown on the target, so line-cutters score the way they should
  • Log misses, add notes and tags, and override session distance and length
  • Touch-friendly confirmation step for phones and tablets
  • See your in-progress quiver overlaid on the target as you go

Tournament mode
  • Run formal World Archery, NFAA, and USA Archery rounds end-by-end
  • Apollo enforces end size, target face, and scoring rule for each round
  • Per-end and round totals, X-count (inner-10), and recurve vs. compound scoring
  • Live match play: 2–4 archers take turns on one device, with set-play or cumulative scoring
  • Final scorecard saved as a tagged session you can revisit any time

Targets & scoring zones
  • Upload your own target images and calibrate real-world dimensions
  • Draw custom scoring zones (name, color, point value, radius)
  • Built-in WA, NFAA, and USA Archery target faces ready to use
  • Edit, replace, or delete targets at any time

Equipment
  • Track multiple bows (brand, type, draw weight, effective draw weight, AMO, nock height)
  • Track multiple arrow types (length, spine, shaft weight, diameter, material, nock and tip weight, tip)
  • Pick the active bow and arrow for each session — Apollo snapshots the full spec on every shot

Sessions history
  • Browse past sessions, filter by date, bow, arrow, target, or tag
  • Per-quiver stats panel: shot/miss count, MPI, σ from center, σ from MPI, group radius, best and worst shot
  • Replay every shot of a session laid over the target
  • Edit, delete, or revisit any past session

Analytics
  • Every report splits the two error modes apart: accuracy (where your group sits) vs. precision (how tight it is)
  • A full report suite — hits by zone, arrows over time, accuracy & precision trends, within-session drift, cold-bore vs. warmed-up, draw-weight tuning, shot-density and calendar heatmaps, and expected-score projection
  • Head-to-head equipment and tag comparisons with proper statistics: Hotelling's T² (accuracy), Brown-Forsythe (precision), and Mann-Whitney U with Cliff's δ (total error), Holm-Bonferroni corrected and target-size-normalized
  • SVG charts plus full data tables, with CSV and Excel download
  • Animated shot replays for any target

Predict performance
  • Forecast hit rate, group geometry, and expected score from your historical shots
  • Filter the fit on date range, bow, arrow, target, or tag
  • Project against any target face and distance you choose

Tools & calculators
  • Six client-side archery calculators on one page — all math runs in the browser, no server round-trips
  • Wind drift with peak-gust handling, sight-mark interpolator, and Easton-style spine selector
  • FOC, arrow speed (bow-specs energy model or IBO/ATA rating), and kinetic energy & momentum
  • Imperial/metric toggle for shaft dimensions throughout

Notes
  • Per-user scratchpad for setup tweaks, ideas, and reminders
  • One click from the side nav — pops up over whatever page you're on

Multi-user accounts
  • Register and sign in — each account's sessions, equipment, and targets stay private
  • Forgot-password flow with one-shot, short-lived email links (powered by Resend)
  • Per-user timezone for clean display of all timestamps
  • Optional root admin for the hosted instance (browse/delete users, reset passwords or emails)

Your data, your way
  • Export all your data as a single archive
  • Import a previous export to restore or migrate
  • Toggle the whole app between metric and imperial units

Built for any device
  • Responsive layout for desktop, tablet, and phone
  • Touch-optimized controls and scaling target panel
