Apollo

Apollo is an application for logging the data of shooting sessions and analyzing performance.
  • Record shots on custom targets
  • Create customs zones on targets for scoring/analysis
  • Track equipment
  • Analyze data
  • Import and export your data

Apollo will always be free and open source, but it costs money to operate and develop. Please consider putting a dollar or two in the hat. Email me (Dave) here or report issues on GitHub.
