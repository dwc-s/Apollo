v0.55 — Press & hold rework: zoom-the-board with lifted aim

- **The mobile "Press & hold" loupe is gone, replaced by zoom-the-board.** The old approach floated a 160 px magnifier in the opposite quadrant from the touch — fine in theory, but a user reaching across the screen to the upper-left wrapped their hand over the lower-right and covered the loupe. Pressing the target now scales the actual target image and SVG overlay 2.5× around the fingertip (via a new `.target-stage-inner` wrapper that gets `transform: scale(2.5)`; the `.target-stage` itself stays put as the clipping viewport, so no layout shift). No separate UI element for the hand to obscure. Deletes ~127 lines of stage-cloning loupe machinery from both `session.html` and `tournament.html`, and the `.aim-loupe*` CSS block.

- **Aim point is lifted above the fingertip.** A red crosshair (full-width and full-height thin lines) and a yellow shaft-diameter ring sit together at the aim point, offset 32 pre-scale px (~80 visible px at 2.5× zoom) above the finger so they're not hidden under the fingertip. The crosshair intersection, the ring center, and the recorded shot all coincide there. Near the top edge, the offset flips to below — decided once at `touchstart` so dragging through the threshold doesn't snap the aim across the finger mid-gesture. Zoom origin anchors at the fingertip (not the aim point) so the finger stays at a stable screen location during the drag.

- **Shaft-diameter ring tracks the active arrow's spec.** Sized to `currentShaftDiameterMm() * (imageWidth / targetWidthMm)` in pre-scale px, then visually scaled by the wrapper. Same intent as the old loupe ring (any part of the shaft touching a higher-scoring zone counts as that zone) but now actually visible because it's offset from under the finger.

- **Finger can drag off the bottom of the target to reach the bottom strip.** With the aim lifted 32 px above the fingertip, clamping the touch position to the image rect would prevent the aim from reaching the bottom `AIM_OFFSET` px. The touch position is now left unclamped during drag; only the aim point is clamped to the image. The user slides their finger off the lower edge of the visible board and the crosshair + ring continue descending until they hit the actual bottom. Top, left, and right edges unchanged.

- **Tooltip and copy updated.** The Press & hold radio and the parent Input label both describe the new gesture ("press to zoom, drag to fine-tune, release to record"); the old loupe / yellow-ring description is removed.
